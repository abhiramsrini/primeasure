What’s New in Grass Valley AMPP

23 March 2025 5 min read Primeasure Broadcast Practice

Highlights from Grass Valley’s Update

Grass Valley’s post, “Latest News from the World of AMPP,” covers new capabilities and partner integrations for its Agile Media Processing Platform. Primeasure supports Indian broadcasters rolling out AMPP-based workflows, so we gathered the key points and added our perspective.

What’s New

  • Workflow marketplace. AMPP now hosts curated templates and partner apps, accelerating deployments for live production, playout, and asset management.
  • Expanded device orchestration. Grass Valley continues to add control interfaces for third-party gear, simplifying hybrid deployments.
  • Enhanced monitoring. Centralised dashboards give engineering teams deeper visibility into distributed production nodes.
  • Partner ecosystem growth. Integrations with audio, graphics, and replay vendors mean broadcasters can mix best-of-breed tools inside AMPP.

Primeasure’s Recommendations

  1. Review marketplace offerings. Identify templates that match your existing workflows—sports playout, news clipping, or pop-up channels—and pilot them with actual content.
  2. Plan connectivity. Ensure backbone connectivity and VPN security are ready before adding remote nodes to AMPP.
  3. Update runbooks. Document how AMPP interacts with legacy on-premises devices so operators can manage both worlds smoothly.
  4. Engage vendors early. Coordinate with graphics, replay, and audio partners to confirm AMPP integration roadmaps before going live.

Primeasure Field Insight

We recommend setting up AMPP sandboxes that mirror production channel configurations. It lets engineering teams validate new marketplace apps and orchestration updates without risking live broadcasts.

Source Inspiration

Read Grass Valley’s original article for complete details and partner announcements. Primeasure works closely with Grass Valley to help broadcasters across India adopt AMPP—from connectivity and workflow design to operator training.
