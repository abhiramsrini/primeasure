Who We Are

The Primeasure Group was established in 2005 with the mission of delivering cutting-edge solutions to the Broadcast industry, along with comprehensive Test & Measurement systems for R&D, manufacturing, and other industrial sectors. Today, we proudly represent leading global innovators in both the Broadcast and Test & Measurement domains. Backed by decades of management experience and deep market insight, we have consistently created value for our partners while ensuring our customers receive reliable, high-performance solutions.
